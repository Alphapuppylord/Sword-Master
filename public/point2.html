The Cats and Me

Cats need to conserve more body heat than dogs, who might overheat from their excercise-heavy lifestyle. This means cats have denser coats, and an undercoat that's so thick it's like stroking an angel's hair. ... They are constantly cleaning out dirt and dead fur from their coat, which also helps spread the oils around.
